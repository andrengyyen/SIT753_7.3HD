About Us

SARA fashion convinces customers with each style of exclusive design, sharpness in every line of tailoring, using high-quality fabrics and always in tune with international trends. This is the path Routine pursues and leads to sustainable development. Established in 2021, up to now, SARA's store system is the "One stop fashion shop" address that provides men with all fashion needs with all kinds of costumes and accessories. Routine's signature minimalist style brings closeness, versatility and enough power to make a mark for the wearer. The products of trousers, shirts, jeans, t-shirts, jackets... are all designed to be dynamic, easy to combine to wear to work, going out or travelling. Routine currently has a system of 27 stores nationwide
